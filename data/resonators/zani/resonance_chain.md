## Sequence Node 6: First Things First? Clock Out!

The DMG Multipliers of Heavy Slash - Daybreak, Heavy Slash- Dawning, Heavy Slash - Nightfall, and Heavy Slash - Lightsmash are increased by 40%. Every Blaze consumed increases the DMG Multiplier of Heavy Slash - Nightfall by 40% on hit.

- When Blaze is lower than 70, restore 70 Blazes immediately. This effect is triggered once in Inferno Mode.
