## Refinement Skill: Meow!

Gain 1 stack of Hiss when dealing damage to the target, with 1 stack generated every 1s.
Hiss: each stack increases the wielder's ATK by 2%/2.5%/3%/3.5%/4% for 3s, stacking up to 10 times. Switching off the wielder clears all stacks. Gaining 10 stacks increases the wielder's Crit. Rate by 6%/7.5%/9%/10.5%/12%.
